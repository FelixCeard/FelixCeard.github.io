About me

Hello!

Felix here. I am 19 years old, studying data science and artificial intelligence at the University of Saarland, and working as a research assistant for the German Research Center for Artificial Intelligence (DFKI).

Having followed the latest research since high school, I believe in AI that benefits humanity rather than replacing it. As a result, I believe in researching AI systems that supplement human skill sets rather than attempting to mimic humans with large and expensive AIs. This is why, among other reasons, I have decided to join the DFKI's interactive machine learning department in 2021. There, I published one demo, which was accepted at the HHAI conference, which (as the name implies) is concerned with hybrid AI and explainable features.


Aside from working on my own projects, I enjoy a variety of hobbies, including playing the guitar for over 12 years and basketball for 7 years.
